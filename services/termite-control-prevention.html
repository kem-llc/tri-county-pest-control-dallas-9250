Termite Control & Prevention

Protect your biggest investment from termite damage with our advanced detection and treatment solutions.

Schedule this service

Overview

Termites cause billions in damage each year — often before homeowners even notice. Our termite specialists use advanced moisture meters and thermal imaging to detect colonies early. We offer both liquid soil treatments and baiting systems to eliminate colonies and create a long-lasting barrier around your foundation.

What's included

  • Early detection with thermal imaging
  • Eliminates entire colonies, not just workers
  • Long-lasting protection with Sentricon® bait stations
  • Transferable warranty for peace of mind

How it works

1

Inspection

We check for mud tubes, damaged wood, and moisture issues using specialized equipment.

2

Treatment

Depending on the infestation, we apply a liquid termiticide around the foundation or install bait stations.

3

Monitoring

We return quarterly to check bait stations and re-treat if termite activity is detected.

Common questions

How do I know if I have termites?

Signs include mud tubes on walls, hollow-sounding wood, and discarded wings near windows. We offer free inspections to confirm.

Does termite treatment require me to leave my home?

No. Our treatments are applied around the exterior and in crawlspaces — you can stay inside during the process.
