Overview

Our general pest control service is designed to tackle the most common invaders in Dallas homes. We start with a thorough inspection to identify entry points and nesting areas, then apply a customized treatment using low-toxicity products. Ongoing monitoring and follow-up visits ensure pests stay gone for good.

What's included

  • Eliminates ants, roaches, spiders, silverfish & more
  • Safe for children and pets
  • Includes interior and exterior treatment
  • Flexible quarterly or monthly plans

How it works

1

Inspection

We inspect every room, attic, and crawlspace to find pest activity and entry points.

2

Treatment

Using baiting, spraying, and exclusion methods, we treat affected areas and create a barrier around your home.

3

Prevention

We seal cracks, advise on sanitation, and schedule follow-ups to prevent reinfestation.

Common questions

How long does a general pest treatment take?

Most treatments take 30–60 minutes, depending on the size of your home and severity of infestation.

Is the treatment safe for my pets?

Yes. We use pet-friendly products and will advise you on any temporary precautions, like keeping pets off treated surfaces until dry.
