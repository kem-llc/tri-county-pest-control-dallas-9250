Rodent Control & Exclusion

Seal out mice and rats with our comprehensive exclusion and trapping services.

Schedule this service

Overview

Rodents can spread disease and chew through wiring, creating fire hazards. Our rodent control service starts with a detailed exterior inspection to find every possible entry point. We use a combination of trapping (snap traps, electronic traps) and exclusion (sealing holes with steel wool and caulk) to keep rodents out for good.

What's included

  • Thorough exclusion — no more rodents entering
  • Safe, humane trapping options available
  • Prevents future infestations
  • Includes attic and crawlspace inspection

How it works

1

Inspection

We search for droppings, gnaw marks, and entry points around the foundation, roof, and utility lines.

2

Exclusion

We seal all gaps larger than 1/4 inch using heavy-duty materials and install door sweeps where needed.

3

Trapping & Cleanup

We set traps in active areas, remove dead rodents, and sanitize contaminated insulation if required.

Common questions

How long does rodent exclusion take?

Initial sealing usually takes 2–4 hours, but full exclusion may require multiple visits depending on the home’s condition.

Do you clean up rodent droppings?

Yes, we offer sanitation services to remove droppings, urine, and contaminated insulation safely.
