Our story

Dallas’ Go-To Pest Control Team Since 2008

Our mission is to provide safe, effective pest control that protects homes and families while respecting the environment. We believe in transparency, quality workmanship, and treating every customer like a neighbor.

Tri-County Pest Control was founded in 2008 by a team of local pest management professionals who saw a need for honest, thorough service in the Dallas area. Starting with just one truck and a passion for solving pest problems, we grew through word-of-mouth referrals by delivering consistent results. Today, we serve thousands of homes and businesses across Dallas County, Collin County, and Denton County — and we’re still family-owned.

Over the years, we’ve invested in the latest training and equipment, from thermal imaging for termite detection to eco-friendly products that are tough on pests but gentle on your home. Our technicians undergo continuous education to stay ahead of emerging pest threats. We’re proud to be a trusted partner in keeping Dallas properties safe, healthy, and pest-free.
