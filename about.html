Our story

Dallas' Trusted Pest Control Since 2008

To provide safe, effective, and affordable pest control that gives our neighbors peace of mind.

Tri-County Pest Control was founded in 2008 by a team of local pest control experts who saw a need for honest, reliable service in Dallas. What started as a small operation has grown into one of the most trusted pest control companies in the area, serving thousands of homes and businesses.

We believe in treating every home like our own. That means using the safest products, showing up on time, and standing behind our work with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Our team continues to train on the latest techniques to stay ahead of Dallas' ever-changing pest challenges.
